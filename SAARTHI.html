About SAARTHI

SAARTHI is VIT's premier mentorship and guidance club, dedicated to helping students navigate their academic and professional journeys. Our name, meaning 'charioteer' in Sanskrit, reflects our commitment to guiding students towards their goals.

We organize mentorship programs, career guidance sessions, and skill development workshops to empower students with the knowledge and tools they need to succeed.

Our Activities

Mentorship Program

One-on-one mentoring sessions with experienced seniors and industry professionals.

Career Workshops

Regular workshops on resume building, interview preparation, and career planning.

Skill Development

Technical and soft skill development sessions to enhance student capabilities.
