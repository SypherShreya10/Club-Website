EPEC

Event Planning and Execution Committee

Vision

Our vision is to forge exemplary events, entertain, enlighten and inspire people around the globe through the power of unparalleled storytelling, reflecting inspiring ideologies, creative minds, and revolutionary technologies that make us create beyond your imagination.

Mission

The Event Planning and Execution Committee (EPEC), serves as the Student Council responsible for VIT's flagship events, including the electric vibes of the Freshers Party to the artistic brilliance of Vishwakarandak, from the cultural extravaganza of MÃ©lange to our spirited celebrations on Independence Day, we're not just event organizers; we're memory weavers.

Our Events

Vishwakarandak

A four-day whirlwind that ignites the campus with a fusion of talent, camaraderie, and spirited rivalry. This flagship event features competitions spanning:

  • Sports competitions
  • Literary events
  • Technical challenges
  • Arts and cultural showcases
  • Inter-department competitions

VishwOTSAV

An esteemed three-day tradition offering a wide spectrum of captivating events and engaging activities, creating everlasting memories.

  • Cultural performances
  • Interactive workshops
  • Entertainment shows
  • Student competitions
  • Networking opportunities

Vishwa Tech Spectra (VTS)

A three-day inter-college fest transforming VIT Pune into a hub of innovation and tech-driven excitement.

  • Coding competitions
  • Robotics challenges
  • Technical workshops
  • Industry expert sessions
  • Innovation showcases

Freshers' Party

A glorious welcoming ceremony for our freshmen marking the beginning of their academic journey.

  • Student performances
  • Talent showcases
  • Club introductions
  • Interactive sessions
  • Cultural celebrations

Join EPEC

Be part of the team that creates unforgettable experiences at VIT Pune. As an EPEC member, you'll:

  • Develop leadership and management skills
  • Work on large-scale event planning
  • Build a strong professional network
  • Gain hands-on experience in event execution
  • Create lasting memories for the college community
